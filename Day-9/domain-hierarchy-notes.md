## Domain Hierarchy

### Structure Example
admin.tryhackme.com

### Breakdown
- .com → Top Level Domain (TLD)
- tryhackme → Second Level Domain
- admin → Subdomain

### Root Domain
Highest level in DNS hierarchy.
Represented using:
.

### Top Level Domains
Examples:
- .com
- .edu
- .gov
- .mil

### Subdomains
Examples:
- mail.google.com
- maps.google.com
- admin.tryhackme.com

### Important
No strict limit on number of subdomains.

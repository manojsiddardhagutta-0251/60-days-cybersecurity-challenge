## CIA Triad

### CIA Triad
Foundation of cybersecurity.

Three pillars:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

### Confidentiality
Sensitive data accessible only to authorized users.

Examples:
- Passwords
- Banking information
- Private messages

### Attacks
- Data breaches
- Packet sniffing
- Credential theft

### Integrity
Ensures data remains accurate and unmodified.

### Example
Bank transfer amount should not change during transmission.

### Attacks
- Data tampering
- Unauthorized modifications

### Availability
Systems and data must remain accessible.

### Attacks
- DDoS
- Server overload
- Infrastructure failures

### Real Example
Huge traffic floods may crash websites causing downtime.

### Extra Information

### Cybersecurity Goal
Protect:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

Simultaneously.

### Security Tradeoffs
Sometimes improving one area affects another.

Example:
Very strict security may reduce usability.
